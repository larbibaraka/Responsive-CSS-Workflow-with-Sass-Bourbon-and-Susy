We’re brewing up the perfect workspace

Private Wi-Fi

As a TechChopper (that's what we call our members!), you'll have unrestricted access to our private Wi-Fi network. We've got enough bandwidth that you can stream live video, but we know you won't because you're here to work, not watch movies!

Plugs Galore

Need a charge? We've got more ports than you can handle. That's right. For every person, there's FOURTEEN different places conveniently located in your work area to plug stuff in.

Food to Fuel You

No frozen dinners or boxed lunches here. Our chef prepares everything in-house, fresh to order. Our inspired bacon bean burger is a crowd favorite! And be sure to leave room for our famous brownies!

Private Conference Rooms

Need private space for team meetings or even customer meetings? We've got luxurious tables and chairs encapsulated within four sound-proof walls. You can also use our meeting rooms as your private office for a ridiculous fee.
